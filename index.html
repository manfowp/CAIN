The Mission

Welcome to the team. You are a contracted worker for The Company. Your job is to scavenge industrial moons for scrap to meet the profit quota. Use your earnings to upgrade your gear and travel to more dangerous moons. Just remember: communication is key, and the quota is non-negotiable.
