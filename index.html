Contracted by the Corporation, your objective is simple: navigate abandoned industrial installations, secure high-value scrap, and return to extraction before local hazards mobilize.

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to WILD COMPANY ("the Application"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy. We designed this Application with data minimization as a core principle.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or process any Personally Identifiable Information (PII). We do not require you to create an account with us, and we do not request your name, email address, phone number, or physical address.

2. Information Collected by the Platform

When you use the Application via third-party storefronts or hardware (such as Meta Quest or Steam), those platforms may collect data according to their own privacy policies. This may include:

  • Device identifiers and OS versions.
  • Crash logs and performance data.
  • Multiplayer networking data (e.g., voice chat routing via platform APIs).

We only access anonymized, aggregated telemetry data provided by these platforms strictly for bug fixing and optimizing Application performance.

3. Local Data Storage

All gameplay data, including save files, settings, and progress, is stored locally on your device or utilizing your chosen platform's secure cloud-save feature. We do not operate proprietary servers to host or backup your personal save data.

4. Voice Communications

WILD COMPANY features proximity-based voice chat. Voice data is processed in real-time for gameplay purposes. Voice streams are not recorded, stored, or transcribed by us.

5. Children's Privacy

This Application does not knowingly collect any personal data from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal data to the platform hosting our Application, please contact the platform directly to manage those permissions.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or platform requirements. The "Effective Date" at the top will indicate when the latest changes were made.
